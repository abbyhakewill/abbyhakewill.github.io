About Me

My name is Abby Hakewill and my pronouns are she, her, hers. I'm from San Francisco, California and I love to surf, ski, play beach volleyball, and travel. I'm a student at the University of Colorado Boulder in the College of Engineering and Applied Sciences studying creative technology and design. I'm passionate about animation, sound development and production, 3D design and modeling, graphic design, visual effects, and developing technology.
